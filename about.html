Find-a-Mentor is a free platform which aims to connect software developers and mentors in Nigeria born out of a desire to connect mentors and mentees in Nigeria at no cost.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to foster a greater sense of collaboration and inclusiveness in the technical industry by providing a platform to aid the mentorship process of upcoming software developers in Nigeria
